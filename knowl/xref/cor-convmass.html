Corollary 3.2.4.

There is a useful corollary to the transport theorem in TheoremÂ 3.1.3 in the case where the desired function of integration, \(f\text{,}\) is proportional to the density, i.e. \(f = \rho h\) for any continuously differentiable \(h\text{.}\) In this case the Reynolds transport theorem is
\begin{equation} \dd{}{t} \iiint_{V} \rho h \, \de{V} = \iiint_{V} \rho \DD{h}{t} \, \de{V}.\tag{3.2.4} \end{equation}
